Eggplant Parmesan

Description:

This easy eggplant Parmesan recipe has layers of crispy, breaded eggplant slices that have been baked, instead of fried. The combination of crunchy bread crumbs, tomato sauce, mozzarella, and Parmesan cheese is guaranteed to please!

Ingredients:

  • 3 large eggplants
  • 2 large eggs
  • 4 cups seasoning
  • 6 cups spaghetti sauce
  • 16 oz mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Parm
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried basil

Steps:

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F
  2. Dip eggplant slices in beaten egg, then in bread crumbs
  3. Bake in oven for 5 minutes. Flip & bake 5 more
  4. Spread sauce over bottom of dish. Place layer of slices in sauce
  5. Bake in preheated oven until golden brown.
