Red Beans & Rice

Description:

Yummy looking rice with red beans and some meat

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb smoked sausage
  • 1 onion
  • 1 green bell pepper
  • 1 jalapeno
  • 2 cups water
  • rice
  • beans

Steps:

  1. combine ingredients
  2. bring to a boil
  3. spoon 1/2 cup cooked rice into 6 serving bowls
